About Our Business



This is an online business that offers computer and technology related services. It was founded by Velile Sibiya who is a self-taught about computer. He saw a need to create such business to help people with different issues such as, applying for school, funding, a job or even fixing a phone. This business is intended to help people with different technological needs. Later he then saw that everything was too much for him, he then searched for a partner who was going to help him with some of the work he was doing. It was not easy to find a reliable and hardworking person but he did find a partner who was not only going to help him but also offer new services into the business to attract more customers. This business offers many services that are mentioned on the next page.


A lot of people find it difficult to do some things and they need a little help to make their dreams come true. For example, some people do not know how to create a curriculum vitae and they do not even know where to start when creating one so they don't mind paying a small amount to get help. Also, with applying for a school, there are a lot of difficulties one can face when they do not know what they are doing, paying for a service that will help you is a good way to start because you don't want to make mistakes and end up applying for something you do not know about rather than accepting help from a person who knows what they are doing. The job industry is also tough because most of the applications now are done online and for someone who does not have the necessary needs to apply for a job wouldn't mind paying for someone who knows what they are doing to help them with that.
